Sleep Dentistry

Are you prone to anxiety when visiting the dental clinic? Perhaps you're anticipating a surgical procedure where one of the pain management choices involves being asleep. Whatever the reason may be, some of our patients find comfort in snoozing through their dental procedures, and that's why we provide sleep dentistry.

In our dental clinic, patients have the option to undergo dental treatments while comfortably asleep within our practice, eliminating the need for a hospital visit. We collaborate with skilled and experienced anesthetists to ensure a straightforward and comfortable journey.

Sleep Dentistry Services cater to:

⦁ Individuals with dental anxiety or nervousness.
⦁ Those with a phobia of needles.
⦁ Patients undergoing complex treatments like dental implants or wisdom tooth removal.
⦁ Individuals who encounter challenges with local anesthetics.
⦁ Patients seeking to have extensive dental work completed efficiently in a shorter duration.

Sleep dentistry is well-suited for a variety of treatments, including but not limited to:

⦁ Dental Implants:
Providing a relaxed and comfortable environment for the precise placement of dental implants.

⦁ Multiple Fillings:
Offering a serene experience for patients requiring multiple fillings in a single session.

⦁ Root Canal Treatment:
Ensuring a calm and pain-free atmosphere for root canal procedures.

⦁ Wisdom Tooth Removal:
Facilitating a tranquil setting for the extraction of wisdom teeth.

⦁ Children Dentistry:
Extending the benefits of sleep dentistry to pediatric patients, ensuring a stress-free experience for children's dental treatments.

These services are designed to enhance patient comfort and promote a positive dental experience across various treatments.

Benefits of Sleep Dentistry:

⦁ Anxiety-Free Experience: Patients enjoy a procedure without fear or the retention of any memory related to the treatment.

⦁ Convenient Location: All procedures are conducted within the comfort of our practice, eliminating the need for additional travel.

⦁ Time and Cost Efficiency: Multiple treatments can be efficiently performed in a single session, saving both time and money for the patient.

⦁ Local Dentist Expertise: Sleep dentistry allows for comprehensive treatments by your local dentist, eliminating the necessity to visit a specialist for the procedures

Sleep Dentistry Procedure Overview:

The sleep dentistry procedure incorporates the use of general anesthesia for patients who are in good health. A registered specialist anesthetist administers the anesthesia through a small plastic tube connected to a needle in the arm or hand. This professional is responsible for managing the appropriate medications and monitoring the entire procedure.

In addition to general anesthesia, inhalation sedation is offered as an alternative or in combination, specifically designed for patients with needle-related anxiety. Our aim is to tailor the most suitable solution to meet individual needs, ensuring an anxiety-free environment for the patient.

Explore an excellent experience with sleep dentistry by following these helpful tips.

Valuable Tips for a Positive Sleep Dentistry Experience

Achieving an excellent experience with sleep dentistry is possible by following these helpful tips:

⦁ Painless Injections:
Injections should be painless when administered patiently by a professional.

⦁ Consultation Booking:
Before scheduling a procedure, consider booking a consultation. This allows for a discussion of concerns and queries with the dentist, fostering trust and understanding.

⦁ Explore Dental Anxiety Medication:
Investigate the option of dental anxiety medication. Even a mild oral sedative can induce relaxation before the commencement of a procedure.

⦁ Trust in Professional Expertise:
Always bear in mind that a seasoned sleep dentist at 32-smiles Dental possesses significant experience in handling patients with various phobias, ensuring a comfortable and stress-free environment.

Who Should opt for Sleep Dentistry?

Don't let fear compromise your well-being. The nearby sleep dentistry service is ideal for individuals:

⦁ Experiencing anxiety during dental visits
⦁ Having had negative dental experiences in the past
⦁ Fearful of dental tools and procedures
⦁ Struggling to achieve numbness
⦁ Experiencing sensitivity during regular cleanings
⦁ Seeking multiple dental treatments in a short duration
⦁ Disliking noises or smells associated with dental care

Sleep Dentistry FaQs

What is Sleep Dentistry?
Sleep dentistry, or sedative dentistry, involves creating a relaxed and anxiety-free environment for individuals who experience dental phobia. This approach is designed to address dental fears and facilitate the completion of necessary dental treatments, promoting overall oral health, and ensuring healthy teeth and gums.
Is Sleep Dentistry Safe?
Indeed, sleep dentistry is entirely safe when conducted by a proficient dentist. It is recommended that individuals who are obese or experiencing obstructive sleep apnea consult with their doctor before opting for sedative dentistry to ensure the utmost safety and suitability for their specific health considerations.
How does sleep dentistry work?
Sleep dentistry involves administering a moderate level of sedation, keeping you technically awake during the procedure. In certain instances, patients may feel numbness to pain without losing consciousness.
What does "twilight sleep" mean in dentistry?
"Twilight sleep" in dentistry refers to the intermediate stage between being fully awake with no sedation and coming out of a general anesthetic state. During this phase, the patient retains the ability to carry out basic tasks, such as opening and closing the mouth, to facilitate the dentist's work.
How is sleep dentistry performed?
At 32-smiles Dental, we employ sedation techniques to induce a relaxed state in our patients during dental procedures. In certain instances, we utilize laughing gas or nitrous oxide to induce sleep. Rest assured, we prioritize the safety of the entire process to ensure a secure experience for our patients.
